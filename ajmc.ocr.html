ajmc.ocr package¶

ajmc.ocr is a package for optical character recognition (OCR) of historical documents. It is very work-in-progress and is not meant to be used as a library yet. Methods, classes and functions are subject to change at any time and are mainly implemented around experiments (notably in ocr.pytorch and ocr.tesseract).
